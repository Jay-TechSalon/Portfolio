How I Smashed into the World of Programming

When looking from the outside, the glass that serperates you from what your looking at seems to be insubstantial. Then as you continue your understanding and developement there is that moment when you realize that pane of glass.

That glass is the seperation we ourselves need to either smash through or figure out how to get around. When I decided to join Code Fellows, that day I set into motion the smashing of the glass.

Why is getting through this invisible yet susbstanial divider such a powerful thing for me? It allows complete “immersion” into the mind space and the realm of the programmer.

The difference is falling asleep tired as hell because you had to code that next step toward a better product. Awaking knowing there is at least 10-12 more hours today to push forward. Living code and creating a better experience.
